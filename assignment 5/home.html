Introduction

Three sentencess Max.

Peruade reader you have novel, interesting question(s) and Hypothesis. Resist urge to use all the White space

materials and Methods

Four sentences Max.

if viewer truely wants to know gruesome details, they'll ask or email you.

Sometimes adding a pic is good

Results

Highlight your LARGE photographs, charts,maps, or in this central arena.

Don't include every graphic you've made that relates to project. Choose one. Or two. And separate graphics with plenty of white space

if you have just one or two simple graphics, viewers will be drawn to explore them. if you have too many or they are too complicated, visitors will be repelled

Annotate graphics with arrows and callout boxes so that viewer is visually led through how hypothesis is addressed. The goal is to enable to understand the logic behind your conclusion without you needing to be there.

Keep font size of all text (even graph labels) as big or bigger than in rest of poster.

conclusions

Explore why outcome is interesting. Don't assume it's obvious. Three sentences max
